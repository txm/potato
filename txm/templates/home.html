About this blog

"Make us an ultra-simple blogging tool (the simpler the better) with create, edit, delete functionality for articles (using Django on App Engine).

Feel free to follow online examples for inspiration but we'd appreciate it if you write your own solution, so that we can assess your coding style. Also, feel free to add to the brief as you see fit, to make it your own."
